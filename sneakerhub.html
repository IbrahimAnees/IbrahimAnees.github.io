Sneaker Hub

Full-stack Android app that allows users to buy/browse sneakers.

Tech stack: Android Studio (Java), Firestore database

Repo: https://github.com/ibrahimanees/sneaker-hub/

More than a sneaker app.

Sneaker Hub aims to provide a community-like experience for all sneaker-lovers. Featured sneakers are ordered by ratings of users world-wide.

Browse by category.

Sneaker Hub allows users to browse sneakers by four different categories (brands): Air Jordan, Nike, Adidas and Vans.

All the details you need.

Each sneaker's sizing, description, details and reviews are fetched from a live database. Users are able to rate sneakers out of five stars.
