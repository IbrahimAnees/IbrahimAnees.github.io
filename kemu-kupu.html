Kēmu Kupu

Te Reo Māori spelling quiz game.

Tech stack: Java, JavaFX, Linux, Bash, Festival Speech System

Repo: https://github.com/ibrahimanees/kemu-kupu/

Learn Te Reo Māori easily.

Kēmu Kupu allows users to practice their spelling of Te Reo Māori words through an interactive game designed for Linux.

Select your theme.

Kēmu Kupu lets users choose from over a dozen common themes of words.

Start spelling.

Kēmu Kupu utilises the Festival Speech System to speak out Te Reo words, which users can then attempt to spell. The speed of speech can be adjusted in settings.

Get a score.

At the end of each quiz, users are presented a score that is calculated based on the number of words correct, and the time taken. Users are also shown words that they mastered, faulted and failed.

Compete with friends.

Scores can be saved under custom names. Each theme of words has a leaderboard, for you to see how you rank against other users.
